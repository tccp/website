Abstracts

MWTCC welcomes contributed talks and posters. Submissions from graduate students and postdocs are particularly encouraged. There will be poster prizes!

If you plan to contribute a talk or poster:

  • E-mail Elaine Springel (ems (at) pitt (dot) edu) with subject "MWTCC TALK" or "MWTCC POSTER"
  • Attach your abstract as a single-page document in PDF format, font size 11 or 12 point and 1 inch margins.
  • It is the author’s responsibility to make sure the abstract is formatted legibly. Abstracts will be printed in the program handbook without editing.

Submission deadline was May 1, 2016.
